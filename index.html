OUR STORY

One counter. One promise.

Kross One started as a single counter at Lugogo Mall with a simple rule: sell only what we would buy ourselves — sealed, original, warranted. The rule never changed. The shelves just got longer.

01

Why we exist

Buying electronics in Kampala too often means gambling — refurbished sold as new, warranties that vanish, prices that change with your face. We opened Kross One to remove the gamble.

02

How we work

Official channels only. Every serial number verifiable before you pay. Every sale with a receipt and a real warranty we handle ourselves — you never chase a supplier.

03

Where we're going

A store built in Uganda that stands next to the best in the world — in service, in honesty, in taste. This site is step one; nationwide delivery is next.
